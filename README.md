# Dano-Airline

## Introduction:
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Dano Airlines customer satisfaction, flight delays, and service performance using data visualization and business insights. The dashboard shows that Dano airlines had a total of 129,880 passenger out of which 56.6% was not satisfied with the quality of service offered, majority of which are first time customers and also customers on Business trips. Passengers experienced delay for 15 minutes which might be a significant impact for first time passengers on business trips. The objective was to identify service gaps and provide actionable recommendations.


The dashboard was developed to answer the following question

•	How satisfied are customers overall?

•	Which service area need improvement?

•	Does delay affect customers satisfaction?

Key Insights

•	Economy class are the most dissatisfied

•	First time customers have a 76% dissatisfaction rate

•	Personal travelers are not satisfied as compared to business travelers.

Business Recommendations 

•	Improve Economy Class Experience

•	Improve Digital Services

•	Improve First time customers experience

•	Improve service for passengers travelling for personal reasons

In conclusion the data obtained clearly shows that Dano airlines is facing a satisfaction crisis with over 56% dissatisfied, by prioritizing the above Dano airline is sure to make a stronger come back with more returning and loyal customers.
